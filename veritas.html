WHAT IS A DEEPFAKE? INTEL
Deepfakes are AI-synthesized or AI-manipulated media — images, videos, or audio — designed to appear authentic. They use deep learning models (GANs, diffusion models) to replace, alter, or generate faces, voices, and scenes.
Common uses include political disinformation, non-consensual intimate imagery, financial fraud, and identity theft. VERITAS analyzes visual, metadata, and contextual signals to estimate authenticity.
DETECTION SIGNALS
👁️
FACIAL ARTIFACTS
Blurring at face edges, unnatural blinking patterns, asymmetric lighting on skin, teeth/hair inconsistencies, and ear/neck boundary glitches.
🌊
GENERATIVE NOISE PATTERNS
AI-generated images often show spectral fingerprints — repeating noise patterns invisible to the eye but detectable in frequency analysis.
📐
METADATA INCONSISTENCIES
Missing or stripped EXIF data, mismatched camera models, impossible timestamps, and GPS coordinates that don't match claimed location.
🎭
CONTEXTUAL SIGNALS
Background elements that don't match the scene, impossible physics (shadows in wrong direction), and environmental inconsistencies.
🎙️
AUDIO CLONING TELLS
Unnatural prosody, robotic cadence between words, background noise that cuts in/out unnaturally, and lack of breathing sounds.
🔢
COMPRESSION ARTIFACTS
Double-compression signatures from re-encoding, inconsistent compression levels in different image regions, and unusual DCT coefficient distributions.
SCORE INTERPRETATION
0–30 — LIKELY AUTHENTIC. Low manipulation indicators. Consistent metadata, natural visual patterns.
31–65 — SUSPECT. Ambiguous signals. May be edited, filtered, or low-quality source. Verify independently.
66–100 — HIGH RISK. Strong manipulation indicators. Treat as synthetic until proven otherwise.
LIMITATIONS
VERITAS uses AI-powered analysis of metadata, visual signals, and contextual reasoning. It does not run pixel-level forensic models on the server. Results are probabilistic — not legal proof. Always corroborate with multiple verification tools for high-stakes decisions. No tool can detect all deepfakes with 100% certainty.
